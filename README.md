# The Inherited Constraint

**The Inherited Constraint: Biological Throughput Limits Shape the Information Structure of Human Language and, Through It, AI**

**Paper 6 in the FONS Constraint / Throughput Basin series**

---

## Quick Summary

Shows that AI's ~4.4 bits/token convergence coincides with the biological basin, despite silicon having no thermodynamic constraint (Regime B). Seven-corpus experiments demonstrate AI inherits this structure from training data generated by biological brains.

**Key Finding:** Shuffling text destroys structure and doubles BPT (~4.4 → ~10.8 bits). Syntax contributes ~6.7 bits of predictability. The basin propagates: thermodynamics → biology → cognition → language → AI.

## About This Series

The FONS (Fons Optimal Numerus Signorum) constraint and Throughput Basin papers investigate whether serial decoding systems — from ribosomes to language models — converge to fundamental information-processing limits. The series combines theoretical derivation, computational experiment, and cross-substrate comparison.
